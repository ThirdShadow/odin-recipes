Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Pasta with bolognese sauce, which contains mince, tomatos, pepper and onion.

Ingredients

  • Mince (500g)
  • Red pepper (1)
  • Onion (1)
  • Garlic (1 clove)
  • Chopped tomatos (1 tin)
  • Tomato purèe (small spoonful)
  • Salt and pepper (seasoning)
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan with olive oil at medium. At the same time, slice the pepper and the onion.
  2. When the pan has heated up, put the onion and pepper in with the garlic and wait for a few minutes until soft.
  3. Raise the heat slightly, put in the mince and cook for roughly 10 minutes, until it is brown all over.
  4. Add the tin of tomatos and the purèe and mix together.
  5. Season the bolognese, wait for it to boil and then lower the heat and let it simmer for 20 minutes. At the same time, boil spaghetti in a saucepan and drain when done. Serve with the bolognese.
